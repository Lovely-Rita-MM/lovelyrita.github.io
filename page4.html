I Made Cookies Today!

Published on: October 29, 1988

About the Cookies:

I was feeling sad because today would have been sixteen years being friends with Lucille so I decided to bake her favorite cookies since I can't see her anymore. Anyways, here's the recipe

My Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 113g of softened butter
  • 70g of granulated sugar
  • 115g of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 7.4ml of vanilla extract
  • 184g of all purpose flour
  • 9g of baking soda
  • 6g of salt
  • 265g of chocolate chips

Instructions:

  • mix butter and sugars until smooth
  • mix egg and vanilla until fluffy
  • stir flour, baking soda, and salt to combine
  • Add chocolate chips
  • make 12-13 balls of dough
  • bake at 177°C for 12-14 mins.
  • ENJOY!
