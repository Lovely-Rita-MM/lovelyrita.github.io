A Poem for Lucille

Published on: November 13, 1988

Intro:

My dearest, closest friend Lucille moved away a year or two ago and now lives in East Berlin. I rarely get to see her and I miss her so much. We try to stay in touch by writing to each other, but the post is so dreadfully slow, that it drives me mad. I just wish she was by my side again.
