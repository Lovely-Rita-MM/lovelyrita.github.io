OH MY GOD THEY LET THE EASTERNERS COME HERE

Published on: November 9, 1989

How I Found Out:

So, this evening, I was enjoying my dinner with Chums and watching the news- as one does on a normal night- WHEN I HEAR THE ANNOUNCEMENT!!

What Then?!

I nearly choked on my food as I leapt out of my seat for a celebratory wiggle with Chumps (he did not appreciate it, that *****...) and as soon as I composed myself, I called all my friends and family to spread the joyous news! I really really hope that Lucille gets here quick... I have so much to do with her! I mean, aunt Helga is also important to me, but Lucille!!!!! AGHHH

I'M SO HAPPY!!!!

I'M KICKING MY FEET LIKE I'M A SCHOOLGIRL AGAIN!

Update:

Lucille couldn't make it. Everyone rushed for the gates and she got there too late. Aunt Helga and her family made it, thankfully, though... BUT LUCILLE AUGHHHHH (I am writhing in despair) I'm going to die now...
