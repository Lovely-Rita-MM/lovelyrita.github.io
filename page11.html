Together at Last

Published on: December 2, 1989

HIP HIP HOORAY!

Lucille showed up at my door a few days ago, and she clung onto me for dear life as soon as I opened my door! We've been catching up and we decided to become roomates so now she has one less thing to worry about now that she's staying here. I'll help her go job hunting soon, and we'll set up a space of her own since we're currently sharing everything

Update, almost a year later

Germany recently became one again, and I thought I should write about it here since the title is convenient. I'm so glad that there's nothing holding us apart now (as in the masses, but also Lucille and I) Talking about Lucille, we've been doing great! She has a job in the bakery down the street and she brings home some really tasty loaves of bread. I'm not miserable anymore. I have great food and my Lucille.
