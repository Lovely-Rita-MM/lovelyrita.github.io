THEY COLLAPSED??!!!??!!

Published on: December 27, 1991

Sorry for the inactivity

Life's just been so crazy recently... Greta is pregnant and wants me to be the godmother, Chumps died a few weeks ago, Lucille just got fired, my father was exposed as a cheater, one of my classmates from a while back reached out to me to show us a time capsule we had made as kids, and all the political changes going on too... Oh, and also I got run over a few odd months ago!

WHAT????

I was walking down the street today when I saw a newspaper and the headline announced the dissolution of the Soviet Union! I can't believe I'm alive for this… I’ve been thinking back and I realized there's so much history happening before my eyes; it's actually insane to think I'm able to say "Yeah, I was there for that." to the future kids! I think I may have been caught in at least one picture of the wall going down... What if I'll be immortalized in a textbook or newspaper or something? I... Just... Need a second to process this... This is too much to take in, I mean... I freaking lived through this!!!!
