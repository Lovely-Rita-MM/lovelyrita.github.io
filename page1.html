We Vandalized the Wall Last Night. Here's the Story!

Published on: November 1, 1988

How It All Started:

So Klaus invited me to his flat yesterday to hang out with our friends and after a fun night of drinking, Gretta, Klaus's girlfriend suggested we draw something on the wall! Hefner and I were hesitant at first, but after some back and forth discussion, we put our coats on and slipped into the dark of the night.

What Then?!

Klaus produced a can of blue paint from a cupboard and some brushes. I think he had some paint left over from decorating his niece's nursery-- Oh, by the way, she was born two weeks ago and (she is just the most precious little doll I've ever laid eyes on!!) but I digress-- We all stumbled out into the cold autumn night and walked to the wall. Gretta started it off with a broad stroke of the blue paint, and it seemed almost like an angry motion. She didn't even make anything of it, she just made a stroke... But here's a list of what was painted that night

  • Gretta- An angry stroke
  • Klaus- A phallus. (Very mature, Klaus.)
  • Hefner- Kilroy
  • Me- A poem about the separation the Wall creates

Conclusion

I may post the poem later if I remember it, but right now I just want to sleep this ghastly hangover off. E-mail me if you know any remedies, please!

Extra Thoughts

I wish Lucille was there with us... She would've had so much fun with us. I miss her beyond belief. Every night I imagine us in my kitchen making dinner and laughing, but she just had to be on the other side. Whatever...
